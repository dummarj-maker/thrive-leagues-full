# Thrive Leagues Playbook (Kid)

## What Thrive Is
Thrive is a family game where you earn points for doing challenges.

## How It Works
- Your team gets challenges.
- You complete them.
- You get points when they count.

## The Draft
- Teams take turns picking categories.
- Most of the time, you pick categories for the OTHER team.

## Rules (Stage 2)
- Do the challenge the right way.
- Be honest.
- If you finish it, mark it done.

## Why It’s Cool
You’re building great habits and competing as a family.
